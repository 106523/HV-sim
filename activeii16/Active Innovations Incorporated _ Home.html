Active Innovations is a leading business solution, systems and technology consulting firm. Committed to innovation, responsive to our clients' needs, and focused on delivery of value, AI designs and delivers innovative solutions to complex business issues.

We believe success in business is built on relationships that are founded on trust. It works because of clear communication and coordination of action, supported by technology.

Our goal is to make substantial improvements in our clients' businesses, which can be measured by their bottom line. How? By approaching the challenges our clients face from the perspective of their customers, by integrating new processes, systems and technology around the coordination of people and their work and, finally, by ensuring that there are lasting benefits through the transfer of new skills to our clients.

Our Vision

We believe that information technology is the fulcrum for maximizing human potential in critical thinking and problem solving. Thus, trained personnel and leveraged technology result in superior decision making capability and sustainable growth in sound operational systems. Active Innovations is in the vanguard for operational implementation of information technology in mission critical systems.



Our Mission

We facilitate our partners and clients navigation of the difficult information technology choices they face. Our focus is on integrating strategy with technology to deliver competitive advantage and bottom-line results. We develop solutions that meet strategic goals, implement these solutions from beginning to end, and meticulously integrate them into existing processes. We consistently meet our partners and client's needs with quality service and extremely competitive rates.
