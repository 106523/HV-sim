Erik Florentz

Erik has been working with computers since he was seven years old. What started as a hobby grew into his profession.  Erik had the ambition to be self-employed since he was a teenager. He served in the Navy for nearly five years and was given formal training and hard experience in Information Technology (IT).  Erik worked with Argenta Systems for five years where he became a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) expert. He founded Active Innovations Incorporated in 2004 and retained a focus on CRM and IT with his own firm.

Erik was born in Minneapolis and reared throughout Minnesota living in Columbia Heights, Braham, and Knife River. He graduated from Two Harbors High School in 1989 and the University of Minnesota Duluth in 1998. He is a Microsoft Certified Systems Engineer (MCSE) and GoldMine Certified Professional, among other credentials.

Erik lives in Maplewood with his beloved wife Meredith and children Carmen, Seth and Aurelia. His cats Esme and Rosa are also part of the family. In his free time Erik is an avid reader and student of history. Erik is a Gamer, and his interests span many genres.
